Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

⚠️ In crisis: SAMHSA 1-800-662-4357 | Helpline 116 123

SoberSteps is built by an indie developer who has been through recovery firsthand. Your privacy — especially your recovery data — is treated with the highest respect.

1. Data Controller

SoberSteps indie project
Contact: sobersteps@pm.me
Data is processed in accordance with the GDPR (EU) 2016/679.

2. What We Collect

Account data:

  • Email address (for registration and magic link login)
  • Google / Apple OAuth identifier (if using social login)
  • Username (optional, set by user)

Recovery data:

  • Sobriety start date
  • Addiction type / category (substance, behavioral, or return to self)
  • Return to Self focus area (e.g. self-criticism, perfectionism, toxic relationships) — stored only if Return to Self feature is enabled
  • Daily check-ins (mood, craving level, triggers, notes)
  • Milestones achieved
  • Craving Surf sessions
  • 3 AM Wall entries (anonymous to other users)
  • Letters to future self
  • Return to Self progress

Emergency contact (optional):

  • Name and phone number of a trusted person chosen by you
  • This data is stored in your account only and never shared with third parties

Technical data:

  • Install date, notification preferences, app language
  • Crash reports and error logs (sent to Sentry — see section 5)

3. Why We Process It

  • To run the app features (tracking, milestones, check-ins)
  • To send push notifications you opted into
  • To manage subscriptions via RevenueCat
  • To improve the app via anonymous usage stats
  • To diagnose crashes and bugs (Sentry error tracking)
  • To test and improve app features through anonymous A/B experiments

4. Security

  • Supabase database with encryption at rest and TLS/SSL in transit
  • Row Level Security (RLS) — you can only access your own data
  • No passwords stored — magic link or OAuth only
  • Sensitive tokens stored in device secure storage (flutter_secure_storage)
  • 3 AM Wall entries are anonymous to other users
  • Check-in data is never visible to other users
  • Emergency contact data is private and never shared

5. Data Sharing

We never sell your data. Data may be shared with:

  • Supabase Inc. — database hosting
  • Google / Apple — OAuth and payments
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • OneSignal — push notifications (with your consent)
  • Sentry Inc. — crash reporting and error tracking (device type, OS version, anonymized stack traces; no personal recovery data is included)

6. Data Retention

Data is kept while your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymous community posts may remain (unlinked from account)
  • Technical logs deleted after 90 days

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access — email sobersteps@pm.me
  • Rectification — edit in profile settings
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten") — email sobersteps@pm.me, deleted within 30 days
  • Portability — export on request
  • Withdraw consent — notifications off in phone settings
  • Lodge complaint — UODO (Poland) or your local supervisory authority

8. AI Improvement (Optional)

With your explicit consent, anonymized content from your journal entries, check-ins, and recovery notes may be used to train and improve the SoberSteps AI assistant ("Patryk AI"). This helps the AI better understand recovery experiences and provide more relevant support.

  • Participation is opt-in only — you must actively enable this in app settings
  • Data is anonymized before use — name, email, and account identifiers are removed
  • You can withdraw consent at any time in Settings → Privacy → AI Training
  • Withdrawing consent stops future use; previously processed anonymized data may remain in model training sets
  • We do not sell this data to third parties

If you have not explicitly opted in, your data is never used for AI training.

9. Children

SoberSteps is for users 18+. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. If you believe a minor has provided data, contact sobersteps@pm.me immediately.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: sobersteps@pm.me
